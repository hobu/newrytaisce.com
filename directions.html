Directions

Newry Taisce is located in Newry Township, Freeborn County, Minnesota. Drive east on Freeborn County 30 (Exit 22), one mile south on 850th Avenue, then one mile east on 310th Street, and finally 1.5 miles south on 860th Avenue. The main driveway entrance is one half mile south of the St. Mary's Cemetery entrance.
